Reason behind this blog....

I started and created this blog to help me share and keep a log on the issues and bugs that I face while programming and developing. Furthermore, I hope this blog will help those who are facing the same issues/problem but cannot seem to find the solution.

As mention above I would be using this as a backlog on whatever programming projects or bugs that I have faced, which would help get an easy reference whenever I face the same issue and is unable to recall the solution or steps taken to resolve the issue/bug. I would say this would my online "gudie" that I will go looking if such a time arises.

Last and final reason, is this will help me motivated on whatever new projects or programming languages that I might startup learning. As I feel that writing on how and what I did helps understand the process and idea behind the project/programming language better.
